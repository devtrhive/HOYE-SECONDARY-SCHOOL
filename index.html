Welcome to Hoye Secondary School Webpage!

This is where you will find all the important information about our school, events, and performances.

Hoye Secondary School is a vibrant public school rooted in the values of discipline, excellence, and community upliftment. Located in a proudly South African Village (Bergville), the school serves learners from Grade 8 to Grade 12 with dedication and heart.Hoye Secondary School gives expression to the knowledge, skills and values worth learning in South African schools. As a result, Hoye Secondary School aims to ensure that children acquire and apply knowledge and skills in ways that are meaningful to their own lives. In this regard, the school curriculum promotes knowledge in local contexts, while being sensitive to global imperatives.

Our staff includes passionate, energetic, and experienced teachers who are proud of their work and committed to nurturing young minds.

Career Guide

NSFAS Information

The National Student Financial Aid Scheme (NSFAS) provides financial assistance to eligible students at public universities and TVET colleges in South Africa.

Who Qualifies?

  • South African citizens with combined household income of R350,000 or less per year.
  • Applicants must be accepted or already studying at a public university or TVET college.
  • Students with disabilities with combined household income of R600,000 or less.

What NSFAS Covers

  • Registration and tuition fees
  • Accommodation (for students living away from home)
  • Transport (up to 40 km from institution)
  • Living allowance
  • Book allowance
  • Personal care allowance

How to Apply

  1. Visit the official NSFAS website at www.nsfas.org.za
  2. Create a myNSFAS account using your ID number and contact details
  3. Upload required documents (ID, proof of income, academic results, etc.)
  4. Submit your application before the deadline

Important Dates

Applications for the 2025 academic year usually open around September and close in January. Make sure to apply early!

Need Help?

Visit Mrs Hlubi or contact the bursary support to guide you through the NSFAS application process.

    Accuracy of Information
    I understand that all details provided in the online application form must be true, accurate, and complete. Submitting false, misleading, or incomplete information may result in rejection of the application or withdrawal of admission if already granted.
  1. Admission is Not Guaranteed
    I understand that applying online does not guarantee admission to Hoye Secondary School. Each application will be considered against the school’s admission criteria, available space, and compliance with requirements.

  2. Supporting Documents
    I understand that completion of the online form alone is not sufficient. I must upload or email all required supporting documents (Learner ID / Birth Certificate, latest school report, Parent/Guardian ID, Admission-form, and Code of Conduct) following the instructions in Section F of the application page. Documents must be clear and legible.

  3. Learner Responsibilities
    As the applicant (learner), I agree to behave respectfully, attend school regularly if admitted, wear the uniform properly, and follow the school’s code of conduct and rules at all times.

  4. Parent / Guardian Responsibilities
    As the parent/guardian, I agree to support the learner’s education, ensure timely submission of documents, attend required meetings, and cooperate with school policies. I accept that the school’s decision on admission and discipline is final and must be respected.

  5. Online Application Procedure
    I confirm that I have read the Apply Online instructions. After submitting the online form I will:
    • Upload (or email) all required supporting documents to hoye.sec@gmail.com.
    • Use the exact subject line format: Application for Admission – [Learner’s Full Name] – Grade [Applying Grade].
    • Submit documents promptly. An application will only be considered once all required steps are completed.
